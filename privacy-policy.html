Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Amazon to Whatnot Pro ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

2. Data Collection

The Extension collects minimal data necessary for its core functionality:

  • Website Content: When you use the import feature, the Extension reads product information (title, description, price, images) from the Amazon product page you are viewing. This data is temporarily processed to fill the Whatnot listing form.
  • Usage Statistics: We store your daily import count locally in your browser to enforce the 5 imports/day limit for free users.
  • Subscription Status: If you purchase Pro, we verify your purchase via email through our Cloudflare Workers backend to unlock unlimited imports.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Product Import: Product data from Amazon is read only when you click the import button and is used solely to auto-fill the Whatnot listing form.
  • Usage Tracking: Your daily import count is stored locally in your browser (chrome.storage) to enforce free tier limits.
  • Subscription Verification: Your email is sent to our backend only to verify Pro purchases through Lemon Squeezy's API.

4. Data Storage

  • Local Storage: All usage data (import counts, Pro status) is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.
  • No Server Storage: We do not store your browsing history, personal information, or product data on our servers.
  • Temporary Processing: Product images are temporarily fetched from Amazon's CDN and immediately uploaded to Whatnot without being saved.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for purposes unrelated to the extension's single purpose
  • Track your browsing activity outside of Amazon and Whatnot
  • Collect personally identifiable information beyond your email for Pro verification

6. Third-Party Services

The Extension interacts with the following third-party services:

  • Lemon Squeezy: Payment processing for Pro subscriptions (handled by their secure platform)
  • Cloudflare Workers: Backend API for verifying Pro purchases via email
  • Resend: Email delivery service for sending verification codes

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

7. Permissions Justification

The Extension requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To read product data from Amazon and fill forms on Whatnot
  • storage: To store usage counts and Pro status locally
  • scripting: To inject content scripts for product import functionality
  • notifications: To notify you of import status and daily limits
  • downloads: To handle image transfer from Amazon to Whatnot
  • Host permissions: To access Amazon, Whatnot, Amazon CDN, and our verification API

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Uninstall the Extension at any time to delete all locally stored data
  • Contact us to request deletion of your Pro subscription data
  • Cancel your Pro subscription through Lemon Squeezy at any time

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page.
